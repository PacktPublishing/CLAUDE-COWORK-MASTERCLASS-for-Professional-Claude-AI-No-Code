TIPS FOR WRITING YOUR OWN INSTRUCTIONS
---------------------------------------
Keep it to 5-10 lines. Include:
  - Who you are and what your business does (1-2 sentences)
  - Any hard rules Claude must follow every time
  - Output path preferences
  - Anything Claude should never include or assume
  - Key dates or context that's always relevant

You can edit the instructions at any time by clicking the pencil icon
next to the instructions block inside your project view.
