LESSON 13 — How To Assign A Task And Let Cowork Handle The Steps
=================================================================

WHAT'S IN THIS FOLDER
----------------------
PashunSports/
  WorldCupLaunchBrief.txt   The main project brief — phases, dates, territories,
                             milestones, and key team responsibilities.
                             Cowork reads this to produce the Gantt chart.

THE DEMO TASK
-------------
Copy PashunSports/ to your computer, then run in Cowork:

    Read my /PashunSports/WorldCupLaunchBrief.txt file and produce
    a colour-coded HTML Gantt chart for the World Cup 2026 launch.

    The chart should cover January to July 2026 and include:
      - Phase 1 (Jan-Feb): Design & product sign-off
      - Phase 2 (Feb-Mar): Production & supplier orders
      - Phase 3 (Mar-Apr): Marketing build & content creation
      - Phase 4 (Apr-May): Pre-launch & retailer sell-in
      - Phase 5 (Jun-Jul): Tournament window & live sales

    Mark these milestone dates:
      15 Feb: All designs approved
      30 Mar: All stock with KitPro
      15 May: Marketing campaign live
      11 Jun: World Cup 2026 kicks off

    Use a different colour for each phase.
    Highlight England, Brazil, and Argentina launch dates.
    Save as: /PashunSports/Reports/WorldCupGantt.html
    Show me your plan first.
