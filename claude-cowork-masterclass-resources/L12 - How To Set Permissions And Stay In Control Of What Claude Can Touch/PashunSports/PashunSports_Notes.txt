PASHUN SPORTS — FOUNDER NOTES
================================
Paul Ashun — Internal working document, April 2026

MISSION
-------
Make football fans proud to wear what they're wearing.
We're not a kit manufacturer. We're a fan brand.
Every tee should feel like it was made by someone who actually watches the game.

TARGET CUSTOMER
---------------
Football fan, 18-40. Cares about the sport, not just the occasion.
Buys when England play. Buys when Brazil play. Buys when Argentina play.
Doesn't want the official kit — wants something that feels more real.
UK, Brazil, Argentina are our three territories. That's where our fans are.

BRAND VALUES
------------
1. Authenticity — fan-first, not corporate
2. Quality — organic cotton, made to last more than one tournament
3. Design — graphic-led, not just a logo on a white tee
4. Community — we're part of the conversation, not above it

PRODUCT PILLARS
---------------
1. The Fan Tee — bold graphic, tournament moment, national colours
2. The Training Tee — cleaner, everyday wear, subtle branding
3. The Classic — heritage-inspired, timeless colourway

WORLD CUP OPPORTUNITY
----------------------
2026 is the biggest World Cup ever — 48 teams, 3 host nations.
England, Brazil, Argentina all likely through group stages minimum.
Peak sales window: 11 June – 13 July 2026.
Stock must be in position by May at the latest.

KEY CONCERNS
------------
- Supplier lead times are tight — FabricFirst needs 7 days, KitPro needs 3
- Germany and Spain ranges are in review — do NOT include in current comms
- Argentina tee colourway must be sky blue and white STRIPES — not plain blue

HERO PRODUCTS
-------------
England 2026 World Cup Tee (White/Navy/Red) — hero for UK
Brazil Training Tee (Yellow/Green) — hero for Brazil and global
Argentina Fan Tee (Sky Blue/White Stripe) — hero for Argentina
