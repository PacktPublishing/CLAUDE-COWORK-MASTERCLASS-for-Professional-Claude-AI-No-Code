# Pashun Sports Social Post Skill

## Structure
1. Hook: 1 punchy line — emotion, match reference, or bold claim (max 10 words)
2. Product callout: name + one key visual detail (colour, design, graphic)
3. CTA: 'Available now — PashunSports.co.uk' or 'Link in bio'
4. Hashtags: #WorldCup2026 #PashunSports #[TeamNickname]

## Rules
- Instagram: max 150 words
- X/Twitter: max 280 characters (strict — count every character)
- TikTok: punchy, emoji-friendly, use trending hooks
- Always reference the team or tournament
- Never use the words 'amazing', 'perfect', or 'incredible'
- Tone: fan-first. Knows the sport. Not corporate.

## Tone
Real fan language. Short sentences. You're one of them, not a brand talking at them.

## Context
Pashun Sports makes 100% organic cotton World Cup fan tees.
The audience is football fans aged 18-45 who care about the game
and want to wear something that feels authentic, not mass-produced.
